About Us

Our mission, our team, and our commitment to your success

Who We Are

10X Wealth Creators is an educational coaching and mentorship organization based in Hyderabad, India. We are dedicated to empowering individuals — whether students, employees, entrepreneurs, or business owners — to discover their passion, build a purpose-driven business, and achieve lasting financial freedom.

Founded with the belief that everyone has untapped potential, we combine mindset coaching, business strategy, and personal development to help our community members create meaningful, sustainable income streams.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform the lives of 10 million people by helping them find their passion and purpose, shift to a wealth-focused mindset, and build businesses that align with who they truly are.

Our Vision

We envision a world where financial freedom is accessible to everyone willing to put in the effort and commitment to learn, grow, and take action. We believe that the right knowledge, community, and mentorship can change any person's trajectory.

What We Do

We offer structured educational programs and coaching services designed to guide you step-by-step through the journey of entrepreneurship and personal growth:

  • Mindset and growth coaching
  • Business planning and ideation
  • Marketing and branding strategies
  • Multiple income stream development
  • Live group coaching sessions and masterminds
  • One-on-one personal mentorship (Diamond Plan)

Our Values

  • Integrity: We are transparent about what our programs offer and what results you can realistically expect. Individual results vary and depend on effort, commitment, and circumstances.
  • Education: We provide knowledge, tools, and strategies rooted in real-world experience.
  • Community: We foster a supportive, collaborative environment where members uplift one another.
  • Accountability: We hold ourselves and our members to high standards of professionalism and ethics.
